How to Play

WOPR has chosen a magic number. Your task, if you choose to accept, is to guess the number within four attempts. If you make a correct guess before time runs out, you win.

WOPR has benevolently agreed to tell you after each guess whether your guess is higher or lower than the magic number. But beware: when your remaining attempts reach 0, you have failed....
